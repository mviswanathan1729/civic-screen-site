What is Civic Screen?

Civic Screen is a lightweight, privacy-first digital signage platform designed for community centers, libraries, churches, and non-profits.

It helps organizations display announcements, events, and community information clearly across multiple screens‚Äîwithout advertising, tracking, or unnecessary complexity.

Who is it for?

  • Community centers and recreation facilities
  • Public and private libraries
  • Churches, temples, and faith-based organizations
  • Cultural and neighborhood centers
  • Non-profits and civic organizations
  • Senior centers and adult learning programs
  • Youth programs and after-school organizations
  • Volunteer-run and community-led spaces

Free for Community & Non-Profit Organizations

  • Civic Screen is provided at no cost to community-focused and non-profit organizations.
  • There are no licensing fees, subscriptions, or usage charges.
  • The Civic Screen application, hosting, infrastructure, and basic technical support are provided as part of our commitment to community service.
  • Our goal is to make digital signage accessible to organizations that serve people.

Core Capabilities

  • Events and calendars
  • Weather, news, and community info
  • Photos and visual storytelling
  • Multi-screen support
