Civic Screen

Privacy-first digital signage for communities and non-profits

What is Civic Screen?

Civic Screen is a lightweight, privacy-first digital signage platform designed for community centers, libraries, churches, and non-profits.

Who is it for?

  • Community centers
  • Libraries
  • Churches and faith organizations
  • Non-profits and civic spaces

Core Capabilities

  • Announcements and daily updates
  • Events and calendars
  • Weather, news, and community info
  • Photos and visual storytelling
  • Multi-screen support

Privacy First

Civic Screen does not track users, collect personal data, or rely on advertising networks. It is designed to respect community trust.
